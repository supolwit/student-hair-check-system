Google Sheet structure (recommended):

Sheet name: Students
Header row (A1..): studentId, name, grade

Sheet name: Users
Header row (A1..): userid, password, role
- Example: teacher1, yourpassword, teacher

Sheet name: Inspections
Header row: studentId, name, grade, date, result, teacher, note, recordId

Sheet name: Calendar
Header row: month, year, activity, level, date, owner, place, note, eventId

Sheet name: Records
(optional) can be same as Inspections for history
